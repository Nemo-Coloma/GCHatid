Terms of Service & Privacy Policy for GCHatid

1. Terms of Service

1.1 Acceptance of Terms

By accessing or using the GCHatid ridesharing service, you agree to be bound by these Terms of Service (TOS).

1.2 Student ID and Account Eligibility (Crucial)

  • **Required Identification:** To create an account, you must provide a **valid Student ID Number** and your **Full Legal Name** associated with your educational institution.
  • **Verification:** We reserve the right to verify your student status and identity using the provided Student ID and Name. Failure to maintain verifiable student status will result in immediate account termination.
  • **Student Drivers:** Only verified students who meet all additional background, vehicle, and insurance requirements are eligible to register as Drivers.
  • **Account Security:** You are responsible for all activity under your account. You must notify us immediately of any unauthorized use.

1.3 Rules of Conduct

As both a Rider and a Driver, you agree to:

  • Treat all other students (Riders and Drivers) with respect and courtesy.
  • Maintain accurate and current pick-up and drop-off locations.
  • Comply with all traffic laws if operating as a Driver.
  • Not use the Service for any unlawful, fraudulent, or non-educational related purposes.

1.4 Limitation of Liability and Assumption of Risk

You understand and agree that GCHatid is a platform connecting independent student Drivers with student Riders. We are **not** a transportation provider. Your use of the Service is at your own risk. We are not responsible for the actions or inactions of Riders or Drivers, nor for any damage, injury, or loss that may result from the use of the Service.

1.5 Termination

We may suspend or terminate your account at any time, with or without notice, including if we suspect misuse of the Student ID or non-compliance with the Code of Conduct.

2. Privacy Policy

2.1 Information We Collect (Focus on PII)

We collect two primary categories of information to provide our service:

  • **Student Identification Information (PII):** Your **Student ID Number** and **Full Legal Name** (used for verification), and email address.
  • **Transactional Information:** Ride request details, GPS location data (collected *only* when the app is in use for a ride), payment information (handled by third-party processor), and device information.

2.2 How We Use Your Data

  • **Verification:** Your Student ID is used solely to verify your active enrollment and eligibility to use the service. **The Student ID is never shared with other users (Drivers or Riders).**
  • **Service Provision:** We use your Name (typically First Name and Initial), Location Data, and Account Status to match Riders and Drivers and facilitate the ride.
  • **Safety and Security:** For dispute resolution, incident reporting, and maintaining the integrity of the platform.
